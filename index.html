I'm a Computer Science Doctor

  • Name: IKHELEF Issam Abdeldjalil
  • Current position: PostDoc
  • Residence: Paris - France

I am a passionate and driven Postdoctoral Researcher at IMT Atlantique, with a focus on optimization and security in network management, particularly within 5G environments. My research interests lie in enhancing the security and efficiency of network functions and services through innovative approaches such as formal verification, dynamic monitoring, and advanced algorithmic techniques. With a background in computer science, including a PhD in optimizing Virtual Network Functions (VNF) placement and chaining, I have developed a strong foundation in algorithm design, network operations, and computational intelligence. My work is centered on contributing to the development of secure, scalable, and efficient solutions for future network technologies. I am always eager to collaborate with fellow researchers and professionals in the field, as I believe in the power of shared knowledge and collective advancement. My aim is to contribute to the establishment of new standards for network slicing and security policies that will shape the future of telecommunications. Outside of research, I enjoy exploring new technologies, solving complex problems, and continuously challenging myself to learn and grow. I’m excited to be part of a community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the world of networks and cybersecurity.
